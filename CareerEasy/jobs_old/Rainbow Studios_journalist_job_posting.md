# **Junior Journalist**  
### **Rainbow Studios – San Diego, CA**  

## **Job Overview**  
Rainbow Studios, a dynamic media company based in San Diego, is seeking a passionate and detail-oriented Junior Journalist to join our team. This role is perfect for an early-career professional eager to develop their skills in research, writing, and multimedia storytelling while contributing to our diverse range of media projects.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Research, write, and edit engaging news articles, features, and interviews under tight deadlines.  
- Assist in fact-checking and verifying sources to ensure accuracy and credibility.  
- Collaborate with editors, photographers, and videographers to produce multimedia content.  
- Pitch compelling story ideas aligned with Rainbow Studios’ editorial vision.  
- Stay updated on industry trends, local events, and cultural developments.  
- Contribute to social media content and audience engagement efforts.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Journalism, Communications, or a related field (or equivalent experience).  
- Strong writing, editing, and proofreading skills with attention to detail.  
- Basic knowledge of AP Style and digital media tools (e.g., CMS, SEO, social platforms).  
- Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.  
- Prior internship or freelance experience in journalism is a plus.  
- Passion for storytelling and a curiosity for uncovering unique angles.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: **$45,000–$55,000/year** (based on experience).  
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) with company match.  
- Flexible work environment with hybrid remote/office options.  
- Professional development opportunities and mentorship.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Rainbow Studios** is a forward-thinking media company dedicated to producing high-quality, inclusive content across digital, print, and broadcast platforms. Our San Diego office fosters creativity, collaboration, and innovation in a vibrant coastal setting.
