# **Senior Marketing Manager**  

## **Job Overview**  
Quantum Studio, a leading media company based in New York City, is seeking a dynamic and experienced **Senior Marketing Manager** to lead our marketing initiatives. The ideal candidate will develop and execute innovative marketing strategies to drive brand growth, audience engagement, and revenue. If you are a strategic thinker with a passion for media and storytelling, we’d love to hear from you.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and implement comprehensive marketing strategies to promote Quantum Studio’s media content, services, and brand.  
- Lead cross-functional teams (creative, digital, PR, sales) to execute integrated campaigns.  
- Analyze market trends, audience insights, and competitor activity to identify growth opportunities.  
- Oversee digital marketing efforts, including social media, email, SEO/SEM, and content marketing.  
- Manage budgets, track KPIs, and report on campaign performance to senior leadership.  
- Cultivate partnerships with influencers, media outlets, and industry stakeholders.  
- Ensure brand consistency across all channels and touchpoints.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Bachelor’s degree** in Marketing, Communications, Business, or related field (MBA preferred).  
- **7+ years** of marketing experience, with **3+ years** in a senior role (media/entertainment industry a plus).  
- Proven track record of developing and executing successful marketing campaigns.  
- Strong analytical skills with expertise in data-driven decision-making.  
- Proficiency in digital marketing tools (Google Analytics, CRM platforms, social media ads).  
- Excellent leadership, communication, and project management skills.  
- Creative mindset with the ability to think outside the box.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** $110,000–$140,000 annually (commensurate with experience).  
- **Benefits:** Health/dental/vision insurance, 401(k) matching, unlimited PTO, remote work flexibility, professional development stipend, and more.  

## **Company and Location**  
- **Company:** Quantum Studio  
- **Location:** New York City, NY (Hybrid work model)
