# **Chemical Engineer**  
*Umbrella Engineering – San Francisco, CA, United States*  

## **Job Overview**  
Umbrella Engineering is seeking a highly skilled and motivated **Chemical Engineer** to join our dynamic team in San Francisco, CA. The ideal candidate will play a key role in designing, optimizing, and implementing chemical processes to support our innovative engineering projects. If you have a passion for problem-solving and a strong background in chemical engineering, we encourage you to apply.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and optimize chemical processes for industrial applications.  
- Design and troubleshoot equipment and systems for chemical production.  
- Conduct experiments and analyze data to improve efficiency, safety, and sustainability.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure project success.  
- Ensure compliance with industry standards, safety regulations, and environmental policies.  
- Prepare technical reports, process documentation, and presentations.  
- Assist in scaling up laboratory processes to full-scale production.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in **Chemical Engineering** or a related field.  
- 3+ years of experience in chemical process engineering (entry-level candidates with strong internships may be considered).  
- Proficiency in process simulation software (e.g., Aspen HYSYS, CHEMCAD).  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Knowledge of safety protocols and regulatory compliance (OSHA, EPA).  
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.  
- Experience in industrial manufacturing or R&D is a plus.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: **$90,000 – $130,000/year** (based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) matching program.  
- Paid time off and flexible work arrangements.  
- Professional development and career growth opportunities.  
- Relocation assistance for qualified candidates.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Umbrella Engineering** is a leading engineering firm specializing in cutting-edge chemical and industrial solutions. Based in **San Francisco, CA**, we pride ourselves on innovation, sustainability, and a collaborative work environment.
