# Video Editor

## Job Overview  
Rainbow Studios, a dynamic media company based in San Diego, CA, is seeking a talented and creative Video Editor to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong eye for detail, a passion for storytelling, and the ability to transform raw footage into compelling visual content. This role offers the opportunity to work on diverse projects, from commercials to digital media, in a collaborative and innovative environment.

## Job Responsibilities  
- Edit and assemble raw footage into polished, high-quality videos for various platforms (social media, websites, broadcast, etc.).  
- Collaborate with producers, directors, and other team members to understand project vision and deliver on creative goals.  
- Enhance videos with graphics, music, sound effects, and color correction.  
- Ensure all edited content aligns with brand guidelines and messaging.  
- Manage multiple projects simultaneously while meeting deadlines.  
- Stay updated on industry trends and editing software advancements.  

## Job Requirements  
- Proven experience as a Video Editor, with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.  
- Proficiency in editing software (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, After Effects).  
- Knowledge of motion graphics, color grading, and audio editing.  
- Strong storytelling skills and attention to detail.  
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.  
- Excellent time management and organizational skills.  
- Bachelor’s degree in Film, Media Production, or a related field is preferred but not required.  

## Salary and Benefits  
- Competitive salary ($60,000–$80,000 annually, based on experience).  
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) plan with company matching.  
- Paid time off and flexible work hours.  
- Opportunities for professional development and creative growth.  

## Company and Location  
**Rainbow Studios**  
San Diego, CA, United States  
*Join us in creating visually stunning content that inspires and engages audiences worldwide!*
