# **Product Manager**  
*Genesis Game – Chicago, IL, United States*  

## **Job Overview**  
Genesis Game is seeking a dynamic and experienced **Product Manager** to lead the development and execution of our gaming products. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to define product vision, prioritize features, and deliver exceptional gaming experiences. If you’re passionate about gaming and thrive in a fast-paced environment, we’d love to hear from you!  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Define and execute the product roadmap, aligning with company goals and player needs.  
- Conduct market research and competitor analysis to identify trends and opportunities.  
- Collaborate with design, engineering, and marketing teams to deliver high-quality gaming experiences.  
- Prioritize features and backlog based on business impact, player feedback, and technical feasibility.  
- Analyze player data and KPIs to optimize product performance and engagement.  
- Communicate product updates and progress to stakeholders, including executives and development teams.  
- Ensure timely delivery of products while maintaining high standards of quality.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- 3+ years of experience in **product management**, preferably in the gaming or tech industry.  
- Strong understanding of game development cycles, monetization strategies, and player psychology.  
- Proven ability to translate player needs into actionable product features.  
- Experience with Agile methodologies and project management tools (e.g., Jira, Trello).  
- Excellent analytical skills, with proficiency in data analysis tools (e.g., SQL, Google Analytics).  
- Outstanding communication and leadership skills to drive cross-functional collaboration.  
- Bachelor’s degree in Business, Computer Science, Game Design, or related field (MBA a plus).  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary ($90,000–$130,000 annually, based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) matching and stock options.  
- Flexible work hours and remote-friendly policies.  
- Generous PTO, including paid holidays and game launch recovery days.  
- Free access to Genesis Game titles and industry event opportunities.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Genesis Game** is a leading gaming studio based in **Chicago, IL**, dedicated to creating immersive and innovative gaming experiences. Our team is passionate about storytelling, cutting-edge technology, and delivering joy to players worldwide.  

*Join us in shaping the future of gaming!*
