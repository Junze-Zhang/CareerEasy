# **High School Art Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Vertex High School is seeking a passionate and creative High School Art Teacher to join our team in Oakland, CA. The ideal candidate will inspire students to explore their artistic talents, develop technical skills, and appreciate the role of art in culture and society. This role involves teaching a diverse range of art mediums, fostering a collaborative classroom environment, and contributing to the school’s vibrant arts community.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver engaging art curriculum for high school students, including drawing, painting, sculpture, digital art, and mixed media.  
- Foster a positive and inclusive classroom environment that encourages creativity and self-expression.  
- Assess student work, provide constructive feedback, and track progress.  
- Organize and display student artwork in school exhibitions and community events.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to integrate art across disciplines and support school-wide initiatives.  
- Maintain art supplies, equipment, and classroom safety standards.  
- Participate in professional development opportunities and stay current with art education trends.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Art Education, Fine Arts, or a related field (Master’s preferred).  
- Valid California Single Subject Teaching Credential in Art (or eligibility to obtain one).  
- Minimum of 2 years of teaching experience at the high school level (preferred).  
- Strong portfolio demonstrating expertise in multiple art forms.  
- Excellent communication, classroom management, and organizational skills.  
- Commitment to fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion in the arts.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $60,000 - $85,000 per year (based on experience and qualifications).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer matching.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.  
- Professional development stipend.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Vertex High School**  
Oakland, CA, United States  

*Vertex High School is an equal-opportunity employer committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce. We encourage applicants of all backgrounds to apply.*
