# Journalist - Frontier Studio

## Job Overview  
Frontier Studio, a dynamic media company based in Kitchener-Waterloo, ON, is seeking a talented and passionate **Journalist** to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in research, writing, and storytelling, with the ability to produce engaging and accurate content across multiple platforms. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment and are committed to journalistic integrity, we’d love to hear from you.

## Job Responsibilities  
- Research, investigate, and report on local, national, and international news stories.  
- Conduct interviews with sources, experts, and stakeholders to gather insights.  
- Write, edit, and proofread articles, ensuring clarity, accuracy, and adherence to editorial guidelines.  
- Pitch compelling story ideas and angles that align with our audience’s interests.  
- Collaborate with editors, photographers, and multimedia teams to enhance storytelling.  
- Stay updated on industry trends, emerging topics, and best practices in journalism.  
- Meet deadlines while maintaining high-quality standards.  

## Job Requirements  
- Bachelor’s degree in Journalism, Communications, or a related field (or equivalent experience).  
- 2+ years of experience in journalism, reporting, or content creation.  
- Exceptional writing, editing, and verbal communication skills.  
- Strong research and fact-checking abilities.  
- Familiarity with digital media tools, SEO principles, and social media platforms.  
- Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.  
- Portfolio of published work (clips or links required).  

## Salary and Benefits  
- Competitive salary commensurate with experience ($50,000–$65,000 annually).  
- Comprehensive health and dental benefits.  
- Flexible work arrangements (hybrid/remote options).  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Collaborative and inclusive work environment.  

## Company and Location  
**Frontier Studio** is a forward-thinking media company dedicated to producing high-quality, impactful content for diverse audiences. Located in the vibrant **Kitchener-Waterloo** region, we foster creativity, innovation, and a commitment to storytelling that matters.  

*Join us in shaping the future of media! Apply today with your resume, cover letter, and portfolio.*
