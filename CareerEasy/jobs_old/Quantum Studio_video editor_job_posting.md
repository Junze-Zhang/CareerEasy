# **Junior Video Editor**  

## **Job Overview**  
Quantum Studio, a dynamic media company based in New York City, is seeking a creative and detail-oriented Junior Video Editor to join our team. This role is perfect for an emerging editor with a passion for storytelling and a keen eye for visual aesthetics. You will collaborate with our production team to craft compelling video content for a variety of platforms, including social media, commercials, and digital campaigns.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Edit and assemble raw footage into polished, engaging videos for clients and internal projects.  
- Collaborate with producers, directors, and other team members to align edits with creative vision.  
- Incorporate music, sound effects, graphics, and color correction to enhance final products.  
- Ensure all videos meet technical specifications for different platforms (e.g., YouTube, Instagram, broadcast).  
- Organize and manage media files, maintaining an efficient workflow.  
- Assist in pre-production planning, including storyboarding and shot selection.  
- Stay updated on industry trends and editing software advancements.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- 1–2 years of video editing experience (internships or freelance work accepted).  
- Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro (After Effects and Photoshop are a plus).  
- Strong understanding of pacing, timing, and storytelling techniques.  
- Basic knowledge of color grading and audio mixing.  
- Ability to work under tight deadlines and adapt to feedback.  
- A portfolio or demo reel showcasing editing skills.  
- Bachelor’s degree in Film, Media, or related field (preferred but not required).  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $50,000–$60,000 annually (based on experience).  
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) with company match.  
- Paid time off and flexible work arrangements.  
- Opportunities for professional development and mentorship.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Quantum Studio**  
New York City, NY, United States (Hybrid work model)  

Join our team and bring stories to life through the power of video!
