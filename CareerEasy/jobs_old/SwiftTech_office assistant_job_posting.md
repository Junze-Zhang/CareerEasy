# **Office Assistant**  

## **Job Overview**  
SwiftTech, a leading engineering company based in Calgary, AB, is seeking a highly organized and proactive Office Assistant to support our daily operations. The ideal candidate will be responsible for ensuring smooth administrative workflows, managing office communications, and providing exceptional support to our team.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Manage front desk operations, including greeting visitors and answering phone calls.  
- Handle incoming and outgoing mail, emails, and other correspondence.  
- Maintain and organize office files, records, and databases.  
- Assist with scheduling meetings, booking travel, and coordinating office events.  
- Order and maintain office supplies and equipment.  
- Support HR and accounting teams with basic administrative tasks.  
- Ensure the office environment is clean, organized, and professional.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- High school diploma or equivalent; post-secondary education in administration is a plus.  
- 1+ years of experience in an office or administrative role.  
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook).  
- Strong verbal and written communication skills.  
- Excellent organizational and time-management abilities.  
- Ability to multitask and work independently in a fast-paced environment.  
- Professional demeanor with a customer-service mindset.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary based on experience.  
- Health, dental, and vision benefits.  
- Paid vacation and sick leave.  
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.  

## **Company and Location**  
**SwiftTech**  
Calgary, AB, Canada  

*SwiftTech is an equal-opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive workplace.*
