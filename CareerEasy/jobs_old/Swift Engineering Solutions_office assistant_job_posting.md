# Junior Office Assistant

## Job Overview  
Swift Engineering Solutions is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Office Assistant to join our team in Los Angeles, CA. This entry-level role is perfect for someone eager to support administrative operations in a dynamic engineering environment. You will work closely with senior staff to ensure smooth office operations and contribute to the efficiency of our team.

## Job Responsibilities  
- Provide general administrative support, including answering phones, managing emails, and handling correspondence.  
- Assist with scheduling meetings, coordinating calendars, and preparing meeting materials.  
- Maintain and organize physical and digital filing systems.  
- Assist in preparing reports, presentations, and other documents as needed.  
- Handle office supply inventory and place orders when necessary.  
- Support the team with data entry and basic bookkeeping tasks.  
- Greet visitors and direct them appropriately.  
- Perform other clerical duties as assigned.  

## Job Requirements  
- High school diploma or equivalent; associate degree or relevant certification is a plus.  
- 1+ year of office or administrative experience (internships included).  
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint).  
- Strong organizational and time-management skills.  
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.  
- Ability to multitask and prioritize tasks in a fast-paced environment.  
- Positive attitude and willingness to learn.
