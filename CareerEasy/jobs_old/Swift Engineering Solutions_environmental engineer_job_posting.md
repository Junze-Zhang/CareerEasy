# **Junior Environmental Engineer**  

## **Job Overview**  
Swift Engineering Solutions is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Environmental Engineer to join our growing team in Los Angeles, CA. This role will support environmental compliance, sustainability initiatives, and engineering projects, ensuring adherence to local, state, and federal regulations. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in environmental engineering principles and a passion for sustainable solutions.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Assist in the design and implementation of environmental engineering projects, including water/wastewater systems, air quality controls, and remediation efforts.  
- Conduct field investigations, data collection, and analysis to assess environmental impacts.  
- Prepare technical reports, permit applications, and compliance documentation.  
- Collaborate with senior engineers to develop sustainable solutions for clients.  
- Ensure projects comply with environmental regulations (e.g., EPA, CEQA, NEPA).  
- Support environmental audits and risk assessments.  
- Stay updated on industry trends, regulations, and best practices.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Environmental Engineering, Civil Engineering, or a related field.  
- 0–2 years of experience in environmental engineering or a related role (internships included).  
- Familiarity with environmental regulations and permitting processes.  
- Proficiency in AutoCAD, GIS, or other engineering software (preferred).  
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.  
- Ability to work both independently and in a team environment.  
- EIT certification or willingness to obtain it (a plus).  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $65,000–$80,000 per year (based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) with company matching.  
- Paid time off (PTO) and holidays.  
- Professional development and certification support.  
- Hybrid work flexibility (office/remote).  

## **Company and Location**  
**Swift Engineering Solutions**  
Los Angeles, CA, United States  

*Swift Engineering Solutions is an equal-opportunity employer committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace.*
