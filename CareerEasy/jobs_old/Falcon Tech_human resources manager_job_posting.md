# **Junior Human Resources Manager**  

## **Job Overview**  
Falcon Tech, a leading IT company based in Seattle, WA, is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented **Junior Human Resources Manager** to join our dynamic HR team. This role is ideal for someone passionate about talent acquisition, employee engagement, and HR operations. You will support the HR department in fostering a positive workplace culture while ensuring compliance with company policies and employment laws.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Assist in full-cycle recruitment, including job postings, resume screening, scheduling interviews, and coordinating hiring processes.  
- Support onboarding and offboarding processes for employees.  
- Maintain and update employee records in the HRIS (Human Resources Information System).  
- Help organize employee engagement initiatives, training programs, and company events.  
- Address employee queries regarding HR policies, benefits, and workplace concerns.  
- Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local employment laws and regulations.  
- Assist in payroll processing and benefits administration.  
- Contribute to HR projects such as policy updates, diversity initiatives, and performance management.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.  
- 1–2 years of HR experience (internship or entry-level roles accepted).  
- Familiarity with HR software (e.g., Workday, BambooHR, or ADP) is a plus.  
- Strong organizational and communication skills.  
- Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.  
- Basic knowledge of employment laws (e.g., FMLA, ADA, EEOC).  
- Proactive, team-oriented, and eager to learn in a fast-paced environment.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** $55,000–$65,000 per year (commensurate with experience).  
- **Benefits:**  
  - Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
  - 401(k) matching.  
  - Paid time off (PTO) and holidays.  
  - Professional development opportunities.  
  - Hybrid work flexibility (3 days in office, 2 remote).  

## **Company and Location**  
- **Company:** Falcon Tech  
- **Industry:** Information Technology  
- **Location:** Seattle, WA (Hybrid)
