Gravity Games is seeking a talented and experienced **Senior Game Developer** to join our dynamic team in San Francisco, CA. - Design, develop, and implement game mechanics, systems, and features using **Unity/Unreal Engine** or other relevant technologies.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate game design concepts into functional and engaging gameplay.  
- Optimize performance for target platforms (PC, console, mobile).  
- Debug, troubleshoot, and resolve technical issues across the development lifecycle.  
- Mentor junior developers and contribute to code reviews and best practices.  
- Stay updated with industry trends and emerging technologies to drive innovation.

- **5+ years** of professional game development experience.  
- Proficiency in **C++, C#, or other relevant programming languages**.  
- Strong experience with **Unity, Unreal Engine, or proprietary engines**.  
- Deep understanding of game physics, AI, networking, and rendering pipelines.  
- Experience with multiplayer, VR/AR, or mobile game development is a plus.  
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment.  
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Game Development, or related field (or equivalent experience).
