# **Senior Registered Nurse**  

## **Job Overview**  
Titan Medical Center is seeking an experienced and compassionate **Senior Registered Nurse** to join our dynamic healthcare team in New York City. This role is critical in delivering high-quality patient care, mentoring junior staff, and ensuring adherence to clinical best practices. The ideal candidate will demonstrate strong leadership, clinical expertise, and a commitment to patient-centered care.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Provide direct patient care, including assessment, diagnosis, treatment, and evaluation of patient outcomes.  
- Supervise and mentor junior nursing staff, ensuring adherence to protocols and best practices.  
- Collaborate with physicians, specialists, and interdisciplinary teams to develop and implement patient care plans.  
- Administer medications, treatments, and procedures in compliance with hospital policies and regulatory standards.  
- Monitor and document patient progress, adjusting care plans as necessary.  
- Ensure a safe and therapeutic environment for patients, families, and staff.  
- Participate in quality improvement initiatives and staff training programs.  
- Maintain accurate and confidential patient records in accordance with HIPAA regulations.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Education:** Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) required; Master’s degree preferred.  
- **Licensure:** Active RN license in the state of New York; BLS and ACLS certifications required.  
- **Experience:** Minimum of 5 years of clinical nursing experience, with at least 2 years in a leadership or supervisory role.  
- **Skills:**  
  - Strong clinical judgment and critical-thinking abilities.  
  - Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.  
  - Proficiency in electronic health records (EHR) systems.  
  - Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment.  
- **Preferred:** Specialty certification (e.g., CCRN, CEN, or similar).  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** $95,000–$120,000 per year, commensurate with experience.  
- **Benefits:**  
  - Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance.  
  - 401(k) retirement plan with employer match.  
  - Generous paid time off (PTO) and holiday pay.  
  - Continuing education and professional development opportunities.  
  - Tuition reimbursement program.  
  - Employee wellness programs and discounts.  

## **Company and Location**  
- **Company:** Titan Medical Center  
- **Location:** New York City, NY, United States
