# **Software Engineer**  
*Genesis Game – Chicago, IL, United States*  

## **Job Overview**  
Genesis Game is seeking a talented and passionate **Software Engineer** to join our dynamic team in Chicago. As a key contributor, you will design, develop, and optimize high-performance gaming software, ensuring an exceptional player experience. If you thrive in a collaborative, innovative environment and love gaming, we want to hear from you!  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Design, develop, and maintain scalable game systems and features using modern programming languages and frameworks.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (artists, designers, QA) to implement gameplay mechanics and optimize performance.  
- Debug and resolve technical issues across multiple platforms (PC, console, mobile).  
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code following best practices.  
- Participate in code reviews, architecture discussions, and sprint planning.  
- Stay updated with industry trends and emerging technologies to drive innovation.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience).  
- 3+ years of professional software development experience, preferably in gaming.  
- Proficiency in **C++**, **C#**, or **Python**; experience with **Unity** or **Unreal Engine** is a plus.  
- Strong understanding of algorithms, data structures, and software design patterns.  
- Familiarity with multiplayer networking, graphics programming, or physics engines is desirable.  
- Passion for gaming and a creative problem-solving mindset.

- **Competitive salary** ($90,000–$130,000 annually, based on experience).  
- **Comprehensive health insurance** (medical, dental, vision).  
- **401(k) matching** and performance bonuses.  
- Flexible work hours and hybrid/remote options.  
- Generous PTO, game launch celebrations, and free game credits.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Genesis Game** is a leading gaming studio based in **Chicago, IL**, dedicated to creating immersive, player-first experiences. Our team values creativity, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology. Join us in shaping the future of gaming!
