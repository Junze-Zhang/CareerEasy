# **Senior Office Clerk**  
*Forge Medical Center – Edmonton, AB, Canada*  

## **Job Overview**  
Forge Medical Center is seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented **Senior Office Clerk** to join our administrative team. The ideal candidate will play a key role in ensuring smooth office operations, managing records, and supporting staff and patients with exceptional service.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Maintain and organize patient records, ensuring accuracy and confidentiality.  
- Schedule appointments, manage calendars, and coordinate meetings.  
- Handle incoming calls, emails, and correspondence in a professional manner.  
- Process invoices, payments, and other financial documentation.  
- Assist with office supply inventory and ordering.  
- Support HR with onboarding new employees and maintaining personnel files.  
- Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and company policies.  
- Provide administrative support to senior staff as needed.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Education:** High school diploma required; post-secondary education in office administration or related field preferred.  
- **Experience:** Minimum 3 years of office administration experience, preferably in healthcare.  
- **Skills:**  
  - Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook).  
  - Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.  
  - Excellent written and verbal communication skills.  
  - Familiarity with electronic health records (EHR) systems is an asset.  
- **Attributes:**  
  - High attention to detail and problem-solving skills.  
  - Ability to work independently and as part of a team.  
  - Professional demeanor and customer service orientation.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary based on experience ($45,000–$55,000 annually).  
- Comprehensive health and dental benefits.  
- Paid vacation and sick leave.  
- Opportunities for professional development and training.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Forge Medical Center** is a trusted healthcare provider in Edmonton, AB, dedicated to delivering high-quality patient care in a supportive environment.  

**Location:** 123 Healthcare Ave, Edmonton, AB T5K 2N1  
**Job Type:** Full-time, Permanent  
**Schedule:** Monday–Friday, 8:30 AM–4:30 PM
