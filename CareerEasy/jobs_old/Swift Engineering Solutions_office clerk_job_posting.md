# Junior Office Clerk

## Job Overview  
Swift Engineering Solutions is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Office Clerk to join our team in Los Angeles, CA. This entry-level position is ideal for someone looking to gain experience in administrative support within a dynamic engineering environment. The successful candidate will assist with daily office operations, ensuring smooth and efficient workflow.

## Job Responsibilities  
- Perform general clerical duties, including filing, photocopying, scanning, and data entry.  
- Assist with managing office supplies inventory and placing orders as needed.  
- Answer and direct phone calls in a professional manner.  
- Greet visitors and provide general support to clients and employees.  
- Organize and maintain both digital and physical records.  
- Assist with scheduling meetings and coordinating office events.  
- Support team members with administrative tasks as required.  

## Job Requirements  
- High school diploma or equivalent; additional certification in office administration is a plus.  
- Basic proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook).  
- Strong organizational and time-management skills.  
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills.  
- Ability to multitask and prioritize tasks effectively.  
- Professional demeanor and a positive attitude.  
- Previous office experience is preferred but not required.
