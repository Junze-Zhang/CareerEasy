# **Game Developer**  
*Zodiac Studios – Calgary, AB, Canada*  

## **Job Overview**  
Zodiac Studios is seeking a talented and passionate **Game Developer** to join our dynamic team in Calgary. As a key contributor to our game development projects, you will collaborate with designers, artists, and other developers to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences. If you thrive in a creative environment and have a strong technical background in game development, we’d love to hear from you!  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Design, develop, and implement high-quality game mechanics, systems, and features.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (art, design, QA) to bring game concepts to life.  
- Optimize game performance for various platforms (PC, console, mobile).  
- Debug and troubleshoot technical issues throughout the development cycle.  
- Stay updated with industry trends and emerging technologies in game development.  
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to best practices in software development.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Game Development, or related field (or equivalent experience).  
- **Experience:**  
  - 3+ years of professional game development experience.  
  - Proficiency in **C++**, **C#**, or **Python**.  
  - Experience with game engines like **Unity** or **Unreal Engine**.  
  - Familiarity with version control (e.g., Git).  
- **Skills:**  
  - Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.  
  - Understanding of game physics, AI, and multiplayer networking.  
  - Ability to work in an Agile development environment.  
- **Nice-to-Have:**  
  - Published game titles (personal or professional).  
  - Experience with VR/AR development.  
  - Knowledge of shader programming or graphics optimization.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** Competitive, based on experience ($80,000–$120,000 CAD annually).  
- **Benefits:**  
  - Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
  - Flexible work hours and remote work options.  
  - Professional development budget (conferences, courses).  
  - Bonus and profit-sharing opportunities.  
  - Relocation assistance (if applicable).  

## **Company and Location**  
**Zodiac Studios** is an innovative gaming company based in **Calgary, AB, Canada**, known for creating captivating and original game experiences. Our team values creativity, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology. Join us in shaping the future of gaming!  

*To apply, please submit your resume, portfolio, and cover letter to careers@zodiacstudios.com.*
