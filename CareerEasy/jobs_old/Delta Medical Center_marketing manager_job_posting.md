# **Marketing Manager**  

## **Job Overview**  
Delta Medical Center, a leading healthcare provider in Tampa, FL, is seeking a dynamic and results-driven **Marketing Manager** to lead our marketing initiatives. This role will be responsible for developing and executing strategic marketing campaigns to enhance brand awareness, drive patient engagement, and support business growth. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in healthcare marketing, digital strategy, and team leadership.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and implement comprehensive marketing strategies to promote Delta Medical Center’s services and brand.  
- Manage digital marketing efforts, including SEO, PPC, email campaigns, and social media.  
- Oversee content creation for websites, blogs, press releases, and promotional materials.  
- Analyze market trends and competitor strategies to identify growth opportunities.  
- Collaborate with internal teams (clinical, operations, sales) to align marketing efforts with business goals.  
- Track and report on campaign performance using analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, CRM data).  
- Manage marketing budgets and ensure cost-effective strategies.  
- Lead and mentor a team of marketing professionals.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related field (Master’s preferred).  
- **Experience:** 5+ years in marketing, with at least 3 years in healthcare or related industry.  
- **Skills:**  
  - Proficiency in digital marketing tools (Google Ads, Meta Ads, HubSpot, etc.).  
  - Strong analytical and project management skills.  
  - Excellent written and verbal communication.  
  - Experience with CRM systems (e.g., Salesforce).  
- **Certifications:** Google Analytics, HubSpot, or other relevant certifications are a plus.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** Competitive, based on experience ($75,000–$95,000 annually).  
- **Benefits:**  
  - Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
  - 401(k) with company match.  
  - Paid time off and holidays.  
  - Professional development opportunities.  
  - Hybrid work flexibility.  

## **Company and Location**  
- **Company:** Delta Medical Center  
- **Industry:** Healthcare  
- **Location:** Tampa, FL (Hybrid/On-site)
