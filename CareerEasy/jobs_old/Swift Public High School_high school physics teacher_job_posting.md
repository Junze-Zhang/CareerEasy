# **High School Physics Teacher**

## **Job Overview**  
Swift Public High School is seeking a passionate and dedicated High School Physics Teacher to join our team in Dallas, TX. The ideal candidate will inspire students to explore the principles of physics through engaging lessons, hands-on experiments, and real-world applications. This role requires a dynamic educator who fosters a positive learning environment and prepares students for academic success.

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver comprehensive physics curriculum aligned with state and national standards.  
- Create engaging lesson plans, labs, and activities to promote critical thinking and problem-solving skills.  
- Assess student progress through assignments, tests, and projects, providing constructive feedback.  
- Maintain a safe and inclusive classroom environment that encourages student participation and collaboration.  
- Integrate technology and innovative teaching methods to enhance learning experiences.  
- Communicate effectively with students, parents, and colleagues regarding academic progress and expectations.  
- Participate in professional development opportunities and school-wide events.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Physics, Education, or a related field (Master’s degree preferred).  
- Valid Texas teaching certification in Physics (or eligibility to obtain one).  
- Prior teaching experience at the high school level is preferred.  
- Strong knowledge of physics concepts and the ability to make them accessible to diverse learners.  
- Excellent classroom management and communication skills.  
- Commitment to fostering a growth mindset and lifelong love of learning in students.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary commensurate with experience ($50,000–$65,000 annually).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer contributions.  
- Professional development stipend and opportunities for career advancement.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Swift Public High School**  
Dallas, TX, United States  

*Swift Public High School is an equal-opportunity employer committed to diversity and inclusion in the workplace.*
