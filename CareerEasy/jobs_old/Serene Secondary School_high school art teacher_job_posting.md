# **Junior High School Art Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Serene Secondary School is seeking a passionate and creative Junior High School Art Teacher to join our dynamic team in Los Angeles, CA. The ideal candidate will inspire students through engaging art instruction, foster creativity, and contribute to a supportive learning environment.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver engaging art lessons for grades 7-9, aligned with state and school standards.  
- Teach a variety of art techniques, including drawing, painting, sculpture, and digital media.  
- Foster a positive and inclusive classroom environment that encourages student creativity and self-expression.  
- Assess student progress through projects, critiques, and portfolio reviews.  
- Organize and display student artwork in school exhibitions and events.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to integrate art into cross-curricular projects.  
- Maintain art supplies and classroom equipment in good condition.  
- Participate in faculty meetings, professional development, and school events.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Art Education, Fine Arts, or a related field (Master’s preferred).  
- Valid California teaching credential (or eligibility to obtain one).  
- Prior teaching experience, preferably at the middle school level.  
- Strong knowledge of art history, techniques, and contemporary practices.  
- Excellent communication and classroom management skills.  
- Ability to inspire and motivate diverse learners.  
- Proficiency in digital art tools (e.g., Adobe Creative Suite) is a plus.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $50,000 - $65,000 per year, based on experience.  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer matching.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.  
- Professional development opportunities.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Serene Secondary School**  
Los Angeles, CA, United States  
*A welcoming and innovative high school dedicated to nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and academic excellence.*
