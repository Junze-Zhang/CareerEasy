# **Junior High School Geography Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Vertex High School is seeking an enthusiastic and dedicated Junior High School Geography Teacher to join our team in Oakland, CA. The ideal candidate will have a passion for geography, a commitment to student success, and the ability to create engaging lesson plans that inspire young learners.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver dynamic geography lessons aligned with state and school standards.  
- Foster a positive and inclusive classroom environment that encourages student participation.  
- Assess student progress through assignments, quizzes, and exams, providing constructive feedback.  
- Incorporate technology and hands-on activities to enhance learning experiences.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to develop interdisciplinary projects and school-wide initiatives.  
- Communicate effectively with students, parents, and staff regarding academic progress and classroom expectations.  
- Participate in professional development opportunities and school events.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Geography, Education, or a related field (Master’s preferred).  
- Valid California teaching credential or willingness to obtain one.  
- Prior teaching experience (student teaching or substitute teaching acceptable).  
- Strong knowledge of world geography, physical systems, and human-environment interactions.  
- Excellent classroom management and communication skills.  
- Ability to differentiate instruction to meet diverse learning needs.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $55,000–$65,000 per year, based on experience.  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer matching.  
- Paid time off and professional development stipends.  
- Supportive work environment with opportunities for growth.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Vertex High School**  
Oakland, CA, United States  

*Vertex High School is an equal-opportunity employer committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace.*
