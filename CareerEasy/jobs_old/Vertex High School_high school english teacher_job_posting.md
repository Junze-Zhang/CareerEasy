# **Senior High School English Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Vertex High School is seeking a passionate and experienced Senior High School English Teacher to join our dynamic faculty in Oakland, CA. The ideal candidate will inspire students through engaging literature and writing instruction, foster critical thinking, and contribute to a collaborative academic environment.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver rigorous, standards-aligned English curriculum for high school students.  
- Teach literature, composition, grammar, and critical analysis across grade levels (9–12).  
- Assess student progress through assignments, exams, and constructive feedback.  
- Foster a positive, inclusive classroom environment that encourages student participation and growth.  
- Collaborate with colleagues on interdisciplinary projects and school-wide initiatives.  
- Participate in professional development, faculty meetings, and parent-teacher conferences.  
- Support extracurricular activities, such as writing clubs, literary magazines, or drama programs.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in English, Education, or related field (Master’s preferred).  
- Valid California Single Subject Teaching Credential in English.  
- Minimum 3 years of high school English teaching experience.  
- Strong knowledge of American, British, and world literature.  
- Proficiency in differentiated instruction and culturally responsive teaching practices.  
- Excellent communication, organizational, and classroom management skills.  
- Commitment to fostering equity and inclusion in education.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $65,000–$85,000 (based on experience and qualifications).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer matching.  
- Professional development stipend and opportunities.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Vertex High School**  
Oakland, CA, United States  

*Vertex High School is an equal-opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees and students.*
