# **Junior Music Producer**  

## **Job Overview**  
Frontier Studio is seeking a talented and passionate Junior Music Producer to join our creative team in Kitchener-Waterloo, ON. This role is perfect for an emerging producer who thrives in a collaborative environment and is eager to contribute to high-quality audio production for various media projects.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Assist in composing, arranging, and producing original music for commercials, films, podcasts, and other media.  
- Collaborate with senior producers, sound designers, and clients to meet project requirements.  
- Edit and mix audio tracks to ensure high-quality sound production.  
- Manage and organize digital audio files, sessions, and project assets.  
- Stay updated on industry trends, tools, and techniques to enhance production quality.  
- Assist in recording sessions, including setting up equipment and troubleshooting technical issues.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- 1–2 years of experience in music production, audio engineering, or a related field (internships or freelance work accepted).  
- Proficiency in DAWs (e.g., Pro Tools, Logic Pro, Ableton Live, FL Studio).  
- Basic knowledge of mixing, mastering, and sound design principles.  
- Strong musical ear with an understanding of different genres and styles.  
- Ability to work under tight deadlines and adapt to feedback.  
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.  
- A portfolio or demo reel showcasing previous work (required for application).  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary based on experience ($45,000–$55,000 CAD annually).  
- Health and dental benefits.  
- Flexible work hours with hybrid remote/onsite options.  
- Professional development opportunities and mentorship.  
- Creative and inclusive work environment.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Frontier Studio** is a dynamic media production company based in **Kitchener-Waterloo, ON, Canada**, specializing in audio and visual storytelling. We pride ourselves on innovation, collaboration, and delivering exceptional content to our clients.  

*Join us and help shape the sound of tomorrow!*
