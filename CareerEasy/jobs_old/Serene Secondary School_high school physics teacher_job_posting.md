# High School Physics Teacher

## Job Overview  
Serene Secondary School is seeking a passionate and dedicated High School Physics Teacher to join our dynamic faculty in Los Angeles, CA. The ideal candidate will inspire students to explore the principles of physics through engaging instruction, hands-on experiments, and real-world applications. This role requires a commitment to fostering a positive learning environment and supporting students' academic growth.

## Job Responsibilities  
- Develop and deliver comprehensive physics lessons aligned with state and national standards.  
- Create engaging, inquiry-based learning experiences, including labs and demonstrations.  
- Assess student progress through assignments, tests, and projects, providing constructive feedback.  
- Foster a classroom environment that encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and curiosity.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to integrate cross-disciplinary concepts into the curriculum.  
- Communicate effectively with students, parents, and staff regarding academic progress and expectations.  
- Participate in professional development opportunities and school events.  

## Job Requirements  
- Bachelor’s degree in Physics, Education, or a related field (Master’s degree preferred).  
- Valid California teaching credential with a Physics authorization.  
- Prior experience teaching high school physics (2+ years preferred).  
- Strong knowledge of physics concepts and laboratory safety protocols.  
- Excellent communication, organizational, and classroom management skills.  
- Commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion in education.  

## Salary and Benefits  
- Competitive salary range: $60,000–$85,000 annually, based on experience and qualifications.  
- Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan with employer matching.  
- Professional development stipend and opportunities for growth.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.
