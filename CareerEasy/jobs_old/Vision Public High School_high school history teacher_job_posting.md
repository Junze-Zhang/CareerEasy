# **High School History Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Vision Public High School in Houston, TX, is seeking a passionate and dedicated High School History Teacher to join our team. The ideal candidate will inspire students to develop a deep understanding of historical events, critical thinking skills, and a lifelong appreciation for history. This role involves creating engaging lesson plans, fostering a positive learning environment, and contributing to the academic success of our students.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver comprehensive lesson plans aligned with state and national history standards.  
- Teach courses in U.S. History, World History, Government, and/or other social studies subjects.  
- Utilize a variety of instructional strategies to engage diverse learners.  
- Assess student progress through assignments, exams, and projects, providing constructive feedback.  
- Foster a classroom environment that encourages respectful discussion and critical analysis.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to enhance curriculum and interdisciplinary learning.  
- Participate in school events, parent-teacher conferences, and professional development opportunities.  
- Maintain accurate records of student attendance, grades, and performance.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in History, Social Studies, Education, or a related field (Master’s preferred).  
- Valid Texas teaching certification in Social Studies/History (or eligibility to obtain one).  
- Prior teaching experience at the high school level is preferred.  
- Strong knowledge of historical content and pedagogical best practices.  
- Excellent communication, organizational, and classroom management skills.  
- Commitment to fostering an inclusive and equitable learning environment.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary based on experience and qualifications ($50,000–$65,000 annually).  
- Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan options with employer contributions.  
- Paid time off and professional development opportunities.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Vision Public High School**  
Houston, TX, United States  

*Vision Public High School is an equal opportunity employer and encourages candidates of all backgrounds to apply.*
