# **High School History Teacher**  

## **Job Overview**  
Vertex High School is seeking a passionate and dedicated High School History Teacher to join our team in Oakland, CA. The ideal candidate will inspire students to develop a deep understanding of historical events, critical thinking skills, and a lifelong appreciation for history. This role involves creating engaging lesson plans, fostering a positive classroom environment, and collaborating with colleagues to enhance the curriculum.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Develop and deliver dynamic lesson plans covering U.S. and world history, aligned with state standards.  
- Foster a classroom environment that encourages student participation, critical analysis, and respectful debate.  
- Assess student progress through assignments, exams, and projects, providing constructive feedback.  
- Integrate technology and multimedia resources to enhance learning experiences.  
- Collaborate with colleagues to develop interdisciplinary projects and school-wide initiatives.  
- Communicate effectively with students, parents, and staff regarding academic progress and classroom expectations.  
- Participate in professional development opportunities and school events.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in History, Education, or a related field (Master’s degree preferred).  
- Valid California Single Subject Teaching Credential in Social Science or eligibility to obtain one.  
- Prior teaching experience at the high school level is preferred.  
- Strong knowledge of historical content and pedagogy.  
- Excellent communication, organizational, and classroom management skills.  
- Commitment to fostering an inclusive and equitable learning environment.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: $60,000 - $85,000 per year, based on experience and qualifications.  
- Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement plan options with employer contributions.  
- Professional development stipends and opportunities for growth.  
- Paid time off and school holidays.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Vertex High School**  
Oakland, CA, United States  

Vertex High School is committed to providing a rigorous and supportive educational experience for all students. We value diversity, innovation, and collaboration in our learning community.
