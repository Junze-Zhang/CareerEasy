# **Senior Office Assistant**  

## **Job Overview**  
Falcon Tech, a leading IT company based in Seattle, WA, is seeking a highly organized and proactive **Senior Office Assistant** to support our administrative operations. The ideal candidate will ensure smooth office functionality, manage administrative tasks, and serve as a key point of contact for employees and visitors.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Manage front desk operations, including greeting visitors, answering phones, and directing inquiries.  
- Coordinate office logistics, including supply inventory, equipment maintenance, and vendor relations.  
- Schedule and organize meetings, appointments, and travel arrangements for staff.  
- Assist with document preparation, filing, and data entry to maintain organized records.  
- Support HR and finance teams with onboarding, expense reporting, and other administrative tasks.  
- Ensure office policies and procedures are followed for efficiency and compliance.  
- Assist in planning company events and team-building activities.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- **Experience:** 3+ years in an administrative or office support role (senior-level experience preferred).  
- **Skills:**  
  - Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint).  
  - Strong written and verbal communication skills.  
  - Excellent organizational and multitasking abilities.  
  - Familiarity with office management software (e.g., Slack, Zoom, Google Workspace).  
- **Attributes:**  
  - Professional demeanor with a customer-service mindset.  
  - Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.  
  - High attention to detail and problem-solving skills.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- **Salary:** $55,000 - $65,000 per year (commensurate with experience).  
- **Benefits:**  
  - Health, dental, and vision insurance.  
  - 401(k) with company match.  
  - Paid time off (PTO) and holidays.  
  - Flexible work arrangements (hybrid options available).  
  - Professional development opportunities.  

## **Company and Location**  
- **Company:** Falcon Tech  
- **Industry:** Information Technology  
- **Location:** Seattle, WA (On-site/Hybrid)  

Join Falcon Tech and play a pivotal role in keeping our office running smoothly while supporting a dynamic IT team!
