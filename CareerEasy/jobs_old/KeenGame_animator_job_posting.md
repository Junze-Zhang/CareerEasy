# **Job Title**  
**Junior Animator (New Graduate)**  

## **Job Overview**  
KeenGame, a dynamic gaming company based in Los Angeles, is seeking a talented and passionate Junior Animator to join our creative team. This is an exciting opportunity for a recent graduate to contribute to high-quality game development, working alongside industry veterans on cutting-edge projects. If you have a strong foundation in animation and a love for gaming, we’d love to hear from you!  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Create high-quality character and environmental animations for games under the guidance of senior animators.  
- Collaborate with designers and artists to ensure animations align with gameplay and artistic vision.  
- Implement animations in-engine (e.g., Unity, Unreal) and troubleshoot technical issues.  
- Participate in animation reviews and iterate based on feedback.  
- Assist in rigging and skinning characters as needed.  
- Stay updated on industry trends and tools to enhance animation quality.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Animation, Game Design, or related field (or equivalent experience).  
- Strong demo reel showcasing character and/or environmental animations.  
- Proficiency in industry-standard software (e.g., Maya, Blender, MotionBuilder).  
- Basic understanding of game engines (Unity or Unreal Engine).  
- Knowledge of animation principles (timing, weight, anticipation, etc.).  
- Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.  
- Passion for gaming and a desire to grow in the industry.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive entry-level salary ($60,000–$75,000/year, based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) matching program.  
- Flexible work hours and remote-work options.  
- Professional development stipend for courses and conferences.  
- Free snacks, game credits, and team game nights!  

## **Company and Location**  
**KeenGame**  
Los Angeles, CA (Hybrid/On-site)  

*Join us and bring your creativity to life in the world of gaming!*
