# **Product Manager**  
*Laser Tech – Montreal, QC, Canada*  

## **Job Overview**  
Laser Tech, a leading engineering company in Montreal, is seeking an experienced **Product Manager** to drive the development and execution of our cutting-edge laser technology products. The ideal candidate will bridge the gap between engineering, business, and customer needs, ensuring our products deliver exceptional value and innovation.  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Define and execute the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for laser-based solutions.  
- Collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams to deliver high-quality products on schedule.  
- Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and customer feedback sessions to identify opportunities.  
- Prioritize features and enhancements based on business impact and technical feasibility.  
- Work closely with sales and customer support to ensure successful product launches and adoption.  
- Monitor product performance, analyze metrics, and iterate to improve user experience.  
- Act as the primary point of contact for stakeholders regarding product-related decisions.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Business, or a related field (MBA or technical Master’s preferred).  
- 3+ years of product management experience in a technology or engineering-driven company.  
- Strong understanding of laser technology, optics, or related engineering fields.  
- Proven ability to translate technical concepts into clear business and customer value propositions.  
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies and product lifecycle management.  
- Excellent communication, leadership, and stakeholder management skills.  
- Bilingual (English/French) is a plus.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary ($90,000–$120,000 CAD annually, based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Retirement savings plan with company matching.  
- Flexible work arrangements (hybrid remote/office).  
- Professional development and training opportunities.  
- Generous vacation and paid time off.  

## **Company and Location**  
**Laser Tech** is a pioneer in advanced laser engineering, delivering innovative solutions for industrial, medical, and research applications. Located in the heart of **Montreal, QC**, we foster a collaborative and forward-thinking work environment.
