# **Senior Office Assistant**  

## **Job Overview**  
KeenGame, a dynamic gaming company based in Los Angeles, is seeking a highly organized and proactive **Senior Office Assistant** to support our growing team. The ideal candidate will ensure smooth office operations, manage administrative tasks, and act as a key liaison between departments. If you thrive in a fast-paced, creative environment and have a passion for gaming, we’d love to hear from you!  

## **Job Responsibilities**  
- Manage daily office operations, including supplies, mail, and vendor coordination.  
- Serve as the primary point of contact for internal and external communications.  
- Schedule and coordinate meetings, travel arrangements, and company events.  
- Assist with onboarding new hires and maintaining office policies.  
- Handle expense reports, invoices, and other financial documentation.  
- Support HR and leadership teams with administrative tasks as needed.  
- Maintain a clean, organized, and welcoming office environment.  

## **Job Requirements**  
- 3+ years of experience in an administrative or office support role.  
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook) and Google Workspace.  
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.  
- Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.  
- Ability to work independently and prioritize tasks effectively.  
- Familiarity with the gaming industry is a plus.  
- High school diploma required; associate or bachelor’s degree preferred.  

## **Salary and Benefits**  
- Competitive salary range: **$50,000–$65,000/year** (based on experience).  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- 401(k) matching program.  
- Paid time off and flexible work arrangements.  
- Free access to company games and merchandise.  
- Fun, collaborative work culture with team events.  

## **Company and Location**  
**KeenGame**  
Los Angeles, CA, United States (Hybrid/On-site)
